Wars Of Meta

"Wars of Meta is the first multiplayer battle royal iconic fps game in the metaverse. The game takes place in realistic and modern attraction with other players supported by VR WOM introduces a new in-game currency system which can be used to buy or trade wearable NFTs and play to earn mechanism allows teams to earn crypto.
Players form into two, three or four player squads, select from pre-designed characters and wearable NFTs can be added during selections. Besides the usual battle royal logic which is searching for weapons and supplies before attempting to defeat all other players in combat, players can find or drop NFTs from boxes, trade them in market and the wining team of the game will be rewarded. Are you ready to take your place in the first FPS game of the metaverse."
